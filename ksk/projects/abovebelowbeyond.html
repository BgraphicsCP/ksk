Do you wish Philadelphia had an answer to NYC's High Line?
I'm helping three awesome architecture students put together above | below | beyond, an exhibition of designs for three miles of unused Reading Railroad in Philadelphia. They have grand ideas to turn the railroad into bike paths, public performance spaces, urban gardens and more!

We're raising funds to cover the costs of printing exhibition materials and making 3D models on Kickstarter. Help us out if you have a few bucks to spare, and if not, by spreading the word far and wide!
