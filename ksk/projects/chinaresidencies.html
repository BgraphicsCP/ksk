China Residencies China can be daunting for the uninitiated, yet China is one of the most exciting places for an artist to be today. China Artist Residency Network is intended to help artists get the most out of their time in China. By pooling resources and linking over 20 residency programs across the mainland and Hong Kong, our online platform will serve as the most reliable resource for prospective applicants and current resident artists. The network serves artists throughout their stay by connecting them with their local art scenes and each other. Our member residencies have gathered invaluable information over many years and have extensive experience in dealing with the unique set of questions, concerns and challenges that may arise during an artist's stay in China - visas, funding, materials, interpreters, events, miscellaneous tips and much, much more.
