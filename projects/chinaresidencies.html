I'm on the advisory board of China Residencies, an online platform to help artists get the most out of their time in China. By pooling resources and linking over 20 artist residency programs across the mainland and Hong Kong, we've gathered extensive resources and knowledge in dealing with the unique set of questions, concerns and challenges that may arise during an artist's stay in China.
