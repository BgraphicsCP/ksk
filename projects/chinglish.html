Kira Simon-Kennedy

Chinglish is a linguistics paper I wrote, published in the May 2012 issue of the Sino-Platonic Papers (p.6-25), about the unusual uses of English that still pop up all around China, despite efforts to clean them up. I delve into the cutural and technological explanations behind gems like "save the bumf" and "no nearing."
